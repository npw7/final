Reflection

For this project, I decided to play with different angles of the same 3D model cow. The different angles help to create a different sense of movement in the sliding jpg images, which could otherwise feel stiff. I tried to experiment with different keyframe timing and item movement. The rotating cow nearly caused my computer to crash (it is a decade old and dying).
