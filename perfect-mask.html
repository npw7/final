Perfect Mask

This project explores a creative approach to using clipping masks in Photoshop.

Technical Notes

Photoshop, clipping masks.

Project Documentation

> Browsed through thematic photos
> Carefully selected the subject to isolate and layer mask with solid color.

Reflection

For this project, I decided to stick with the theme of Qing Dynasty palace women. I thought they were an interesting subject for this particullar project, because the isolation in this style of art I feel reflects the often very lonely lives they were forced to lead. For the first image, I blocked out the image of the palace girl. This choice is meant to represent a life lost to time. We do not know this girl's identity, but her image remains. The second image instead blocks out the rest of the subjects in favor of one lone woman. This is Yù Délíng, Empress Dowager Cixi's lady-in-waiting. While typically the original photograph focuses on the Empress Dowager next to her, instead this piece focuses on the figure of Yù Délíng, who stands off to the side, smiling at the camera.
