This semester I learned a lot about digital media. Coming from a more illustration-focused background, I do not have a lot of experience in graphic design principles. Through this course I have learned more ways that digital design can be utilized in art. While I started out this course convinced that I was just not that interested in digital design, I found that I enjoyed a couple of the things taught over the semester. Namely, I found an interest in the more unconventional and abstract approaches to art, which I see as very freeing compared to the more strict fundamentals of drawing. In addition, I found that I very much enjoy the process of coding with HTML and CSS. In the future, I would like to explore this medium more, perhaps through a web design class. This portfolio shows the wide variety of topics that have been covered throughout the semester and aims to explain the approach I took to each project.

Nico White
